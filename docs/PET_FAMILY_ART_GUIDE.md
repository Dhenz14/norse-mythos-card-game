
# Pet Family Art Guide — Ragnarok Card Game

**38 Pet Families | 266 Cards | 3-3-1 Evolution System**

Each pet family has 7 cards: 3 Stage 1 (Basic), 3 Stage 2 (Adept), 1 Stage 3 (Master) with path-dependent variants.

---

**Element Color Palettes:**

| Element | Primary | Secondary | Effects |
|---------|---------|-----------|---------|
| Fire | Crimson-orange | Ember gold | Flames, smoke, lava |
| Water | Deep ocean blue | Frost silver | Waves, ice, mist |
| Electric | Lightning white-gold | Storm blue | Sparks, arcs, static |
| Grass | Living green | Earth brown | Vines, leaves, moss |
| Dark | Shadow purple-black | Toxic green | Shadow tendrils, void |
| Light | Divine gold-white | Celestial blue | Halos, rays, radiance |
| Neutral | Iron grey | Warm brown | Stone, earth, natural |

**Stage Size Guide:**

| Stage | Size | Frame Fill | Complexity | Effects |
|-------|------|------------|------------|---------|
| 1 (Basic) | Cat/dog | 50-60% | Simple, cute/scrappy | Subtle element hints |
| 2 (Adept) | Horse/bear | 65-75% | Detailed, powerful | Strong element effects |
| 3 (Master) | Mountain/titan | 80%+ | Maximum detail | Overwhelming element display |

| Rarity by Stage | Border |
|-----------------|--------|
| Stage 1: Common | Grey/white |
| Stage 2: Rare | Blue |
| Stage 3: Mythic | Golden with element-glow |

# FAMILY 1: WOLVES (IDs 50000-50006)

**Family Theme:** Fenrir's bloodline — savage predators of the Norse wilds. From scrappy pups to the unbound World-Devourer. Fur textures, fangs, and primal ferocity define every stage. Fire wolves burn, shadow wolves stalk, tundra wolves endure.

---

## Stage 1 — Basic Wolves

## 1. Fenrir Pup

**Card ID:** 50000 | **Type:** minion | **Class:** Neutral | **Rarity:** common | **Mana:** 1 | **Attack:** 1 | **Health:** 2

> "A spark of Fenrir's fury burns in its tiny eyes."

**Art Prompt:**
A small wolf pup with ember-orange fur and glowing crimson eyes, crouched on volcanic rock. Tiny flames lick along its hackles and tail. Its mouth is slightly open, showing needle-sharp fangs. Smoke curls from its paw prints. Dark volcanic cavern background with distant lava glow. Digital fantasy painting style.

**Key Visual Attributes:**
- Tiny wolf pup, cat-sized, 50-60% frame fill
- Ember-orange fur with fire-element markings along the spine
- Glowing crimson eyes showing Fenrir's bloodline
- Small flames flickering from hackles and tail tip
- Volcanic rock terrain with distant lava glow

**Evolution:** Evolves into Fenrir's Fury (50003) after dealing damage.
