# Mage Cards

**Class Fantasy:** Ice, frost, arcane magic, and elemental mastery. The Mage draws from Freya Frostweaver's tradition — channeling the frozen mists of Niflheim, the arcane energies of the Bifrost, and the scorching fires of Muspelheim. Color palette: icy blues, deep purples, arcane whites, and occasional volcanic oranges for fire spells.
