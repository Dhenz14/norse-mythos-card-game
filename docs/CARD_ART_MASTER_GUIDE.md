
# Minion & Spell Art Guide — Ragnarok Card Game

---

Each entry includes: **Card ID**, **Type/Rarity**, **Flavor Text**, **Visual Art Prompt**, and **Key Iconic Attributes**.
**Art Style Note for Minions:** Show the creature/character as a half-body or full-body portrait, filling 70-80% of the frame. Background should reflect their realm or lore origin. Use cinematic fantasy illustration style with rich texture and dramatic lighting. The character should feel alive — caught in a moment of action or quiet intensity.
**Art Style Note for Spells:** Show the magical effect itself — energy, runes, elemental forces, or cosmic phenomena. No characters should dominate the frame (they may appear as silhouettes or small figures for scale). The spell should feel like a moment frozen in time. Use the same cinematic fantasy style with emphasis on light, color, and atmosphere.
