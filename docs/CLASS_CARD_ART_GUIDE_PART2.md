# Paladin

**Class Fantasy**: Holy warriors sworn to the light of Baldur, the justice of Tyr, and the defense of Asgard. Paladins wield golden radiance, sacred shields, and divine blessings. Their aesthetic is warm golds, brilliant whites, celestial blues, and polished armor gleaming with rune-light. They summon Einherjar recruits, bestow Divine Shields, and bring retribution upon the wicked.
