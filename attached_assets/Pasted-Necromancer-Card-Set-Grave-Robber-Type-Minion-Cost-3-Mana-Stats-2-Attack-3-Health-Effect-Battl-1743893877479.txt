Additional Details
Graveyard Mechanic: The "graveyard" refers to the last 5 minions that died in the game (from both players), serving as a resource for Necromancer effects.
Undead Type: These cards assume the introduction of the Undead minion type, enabling synergy with cards like Death's Embrace and Ghoul.
Token Cards:
Zombie: 2 Attack / 2 Health, Undead.
Skeleton: 1 Attack / 1 Health, Undead.
Balance: Mana costs and effects are designed to align with typical card game standards (e.g., 2 mana for a 2/1 with a small upside, 7 mana for a board-clearing minion).
