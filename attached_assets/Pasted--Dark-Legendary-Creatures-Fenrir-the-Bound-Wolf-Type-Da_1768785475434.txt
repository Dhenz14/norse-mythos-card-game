## Dark Legendary Creatures

### Fenrir, the Bound Wolf

- **Type:** Dark
- **Mana Cost:** 8
- **Attack:** 7
- **Health:** 7
- **Retreat Cost:** 3
- **Weakness:** Light (+2 damage)
- **Standard Ability:** Rush
- **Passive Ability:** Growing Rage - At the end of each turn, if Fenrir is still alive, gain +1 Attack.
- **Energy Ability:** Discard a Dark Energy: Destroy an enemy minion.
- **Flavor Text:** "The monstrous wolf, destined to break free and devour the gods."

### Garm, the Hellhound

- **Type:** Dark
- **Mana Cost:** 7
- **Attack:** 5
- **Health:** 6
- **Retreat Cost:** 2
- **Weakness:** Light (+2 damage)
- **Standard Ability:** Taunt
- **Passive Ability:** Gatekeeper’s Howl - When a friendly minion dies, summon a 2/2 Shadow Hound with Taunt.
- **Energy Ability:** Discard a Dark Energy: Revive a friendly minion that died this game with +1/+1.
- **Flavor Text:** "The ferocious guard dog of Hel’s gates, a fearsome beast tied to death and the underworld."

### Hati, the Moon-Devourer

- **Type:** Dark
- **Mana Cost:** 6
- **Attack:** 5
- **Health:** 5
- **Retreat Cost:** 2
- **Weakness:** Light (+2 damage)
- **Standard Ability:** Stealth
- **Passive Ability:** Lunar Veil - At the end of your turn, if Hati has Stealth, give another random friendly minion Stealth.
- **Energy Ability:** Discard a Dark Energy: Deal 1 damage to all enemy minions.
- **Flavor Text:** "The wolf that stalks the moon, bringing eternal night."

### Nidhogg, the Root-Gnawer

- **Type:** Dark
- **Mana Cost:** 10
- **Attack:** 8
- **Health:** 8
- **Retreat Cost:** 3
- **Weakness:** Light (+2 damage)
- **Standard Ability:** Taunt
- **Passive Ability:** Creeping Decay - At the end of each turn, deal 1 damage to all enemy minions.
- **Energy Ability:** Discard a Dark Energy: Destroy all enemy minions with 2 or less Health.
- **Flavor Text:** "The dragon that chews at Yggdrasil's roots, heralding decay."

### Shadowmaw Alpha

- **Type:** Dark
- **Mana Cost:** 6
- **Attack:** 4
- **Health:** 4
- **Retreat Cost:** 2
- **Weakness:** Light (+2 damage)
- **Standard Ability:** Stealth
- **Passive Ability:** Pack Leader - Whenever you summon a Shadow Wolf, give it +1/+1 and Stealth.
- **Battlecry:** Summon two 2/2 Shadow Wolves with Stealth.
- **Flavor Text:** "The leader of the pack, its howl commands the night."

### Shadowmaw Direwolf

- **Type:** Dark
- **Mana Cost:** 7
- **Attack:** 6
- **Health:** 6
- **Retreat Cost:** 2
- **Weakness:** Light (+2 damage)
- **Standard Ability:** Stealth
- **Passive Ability:** Alpha's Call - Whenever this minion destroys an enemy minion, summon a 1/1 Shadow Pup with Stealth.
- **Energy Ability:** Discard a Dark Energy: Destroy an enemy minion with 4 or less Attack and summon a 3/3 Shadow Wolf in its place.
- **Flavor Text:** "A pack leader of the night, its jaws claim the weak."
