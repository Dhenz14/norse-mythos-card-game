### 1. Odin - The Allfather

- **Element:** Light
- **Weakness:** Dark (takes 1.5x damage from dark-based attacks)
- **Starting Health:** 150
- **Hero Power:** Wisdom of the Ravens
    - **Cost:** 2 mana (usable once per turn)
    - **Effect:** Reveal a random card in your opponent’s hand and draw a card.
- **Weapon:** Gungnir (The Unerring Spear)
    - **Type:** 5-mana spell
    - **Immediate Effect:** Reveal the opponent’s entire hand this turn.
    - **Permanent Upgrade:** Upgrades hero power to Wisdom of the Ravens+: Reveal two random cards in your opponent’s hand and draw two cards.
- **Passive Ability:** Illuminated Path
    - **Effect:** Whenever you play a light minion, reveal a random card in your opponent’s hand.
- **Description:** Odin, the wise ruler of Asgard, uses his ravens and spear to gain knowledge and outmaneuver foes. His light element makes him vulnerable to dark attacks but excels in control and strategy.
- **Flavor Text:** "In the halls of Asgard, wisdom reigns supreme, but even the Allfather must beware the shadows."
