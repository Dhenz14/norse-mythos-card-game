### Zeus - The Supreme Olympian

- **Element:** Electric
- **Weakness:** Grass (takes 1.5x damage from grass-based attacks)
- **Starting Health:** 140
- **Hero Power:** Thunderbolt Strike
    - **Cost:** 2 mana (usable once per turn)
    - **Effect:** Deal 3 damage to an enemy minion and stun it (prevents it from attacking next turn).
- **Weapon:** Zeus's Thunderbolt
    - **Type:** 5-mana spell
    - **Immediate Effect:** Deal 4 damage to all enemy minions and stun them.
    - **Permanent Upgrade:** Upgrades hero power to _Thunderbolt Strike+_: Deal 4 damage to an enemy minion and stun it.
- **Passive Ability:** Olympian Authority
    - **Effect:** Your minions have +1 attack.
- **Description:** Zeus, the supreme Olympian, wields lightning to crush foes and leads with unmatched authority. His power falters against the earth’s might.
- **Flavor Text:** "The sky’s fury knows no bounds, but even the gods fear the earth’s embrace."

**Explanation:** Zeus’s electric element and _Thunderbolt Supreme_ move inspire a hero power and weapon focused on lightning damage and paralysis (stun). His weakness to Earth (Gaia) is represented as Grass, assuming Grass proxies for earth in the game’s elemental system. His leadership role is captured in the passive ability.
