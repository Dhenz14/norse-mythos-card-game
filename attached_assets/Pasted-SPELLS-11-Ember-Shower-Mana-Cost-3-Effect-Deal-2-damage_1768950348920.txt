SPELLS


#### 11. Ember Shower 

- **Mana Cost:** 3
- **Effect:** Deal 2 damage to a minion and 1 damage to all adjacent minions.
- **Flavor Text:** _"Sparks rain down, igniting the battlefield."_

#### 12. Vine Snare

- **Mana Cost:** 2
- **Effect:** Freeze an enemy minion. If you discarded a Grass Energy this turn, it can’t attack next turn either.
- **Flavor Text:** _"The wilds ensnare those who trespass."_

#### 13. Thunderstrike

- **Mana Cost:** 4
- **Effect:** Deal 4 damage to an enemy minion. If it survives, deal 2 damage to your hero.
- **Flavor Text:** _"A bolt from the sky, reckless and fierce."_

#### 14. Spectral Howl 


- **Mana Cost:** 2
- **Effect:** Give a friendly minion +1 Attack and Stealth until your next turn.
- **Flavor Text:** _"A cry from the void cloaks its allies in shadow."_

#### 15. Rune of Renewal 


- **Mana Cost:** 3
- **Effect:** Restore 5 Health to a friendly minion. If you control a minion with Rune Activation, trigger its ability.
- **Flavor Text:** _"Ancient symbols mend what time has broken."_

### Spells

#### 11. Tide’s Grace 

- **Mana Cost:** 2
- **Effect:** Restore 4 Health to a friendly minion. If you discarded a Water Energy this turn, restore 6 Health instead.
- **Flavor Text:** _"The ocean’s embrace heals all wounds."_

#### 12. Gleaming Aura


- **Mana Cost:** 3
- **Effect:** Give a friendly minion +2 Health and Divine Shield.
- **Flavor Text:** _"A shimmering light guards the worthy."_

#### 13. Ancient Insight 


- **Mana Cost:** 2
- **Effect:** Draw a card. If you control a minion with Rune Activation, trigger its ability.
- **Flavor Text:** _"Whispers of the past guide the present."_

#### 14. Ember Storm

- **Mana Cost:** 5
- **Effect:** Deal 3 damage to all enemy minions. Apply Burn to each.
- **Flavor Text:** _"A tempest of flame sweeps the battlefield."_

#### 15. Rune of Greed 

- **Mana Cost:** 1
- **Effect:** Gain 2 Mana Crystals this turn only.
- **Flavor Text:** _"A sigil of avarice, tempting fate with fleeting power."_
